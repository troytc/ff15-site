Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 1, 2026

FF@15 is built to stay out of your way. No account, no ads, and no selling your data. This policy explains what the desktop app and this website do — and don't do — with information.

Who we are

FF@15 is operated by Kickback, LLC ("we," "us," or "our"). This policy covers the FF@15 desktop application and the marketing site at ff15gg.com.

Questions about privacy? Open an issue on our GitHub releases repository and label it for privacy.

Summary

  • No account required. We don't ask you to sign up or log in to use FF@15.
  • Local-first. Match data is read from Riot's official Live Client and game APIs and processed on your device to power the overlay and companion features.
  • No ads. We don't run advertising in the app or on this site.
  • We don't sell your data. We do not sell, rent, or trade personal information.

FF@15 desktop app

Game and match data

While you play League of Legends, FF@15 reads match information that Riot makes available through the official Live Client and related game APIs — for example, stats like CS/min, gold, kill participation, vision, and team leads. This data is used to score your performance against rank benchmarks, display the in-game overlay, generate post-game grades, and populate your local match history.

FF@15 is not endorsed by Riot Games. Use of Riot's APIs is subject to Riot's developer policies and the League of Legends Terms of Service.

Data stored on your device

Settings, benchmark preferences, and match history shown in the out-of-game companion are stored locally on your computer. We do not operate a cloud account that syncs this data for you.

What we don't collect by default

FF@15 does not require an email address, username, or payment information. We do not inject ads or third-party trackers into the overlay. We do not read your chat messages, credentials, or files outside what is needed to read live match data from the game client.

Updates and downloads

App installers and updates are distributed through GitHub Releases. When you download an update, GitHub may log standard connection metadata (such as IP address and user agent) under its own privacy policy.

This website (ff15gg.com)

What the site does

This is a static marketing page. It does not use sign-in, checkout, or comment forms. We do not set advertising or analytics cookies on this site.

Third-party services

The site loads:

  • Google Fonts — to display the Cinzel typeface. Google may receive your IP address when fonts are requested. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • GitHub API — the download button fetches the latest release metadata from api.github.com so install links stay current. That request does not include personal information from you beyond what GitHub normally logs for API traffic.

Hosting logs

The site is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may collect server logs (IP address, browser type, pages requested, timestamps) as described in the GitHub Privacy Statement.

How we use information

We use the information described above only to:

  • Run FF@15's overlay, grading, and companion features on your device
  • Serve and maintain this website
  • Respond to support or privacy requests you send us
  • Comply with law or protect our rights where required

Sharing

We do not share personal information with advertisers or data brokers. We may share information only:

  • With infrastructure providers that help us host the website or distribute installers (for example, GitHub), solely to provide those services
  • When required by law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request
  • To protect the safety, rights, or property of Kickback, LLC, our users, or the public

Retention

Match history and preferences kept by the app remain on your device until you delete them or uninstall FF@15. Website server logs retained by GitHub are governed by GitHub's policies. If you contact us with a privacy question, we keep correspondence only as long as needed to resolve it.

Your choices

  • Uninstall — removes the FF@15 application. Local data stored by the app in its application data folders may remain until you delete those folders manually.
  • Stop using the site — simply close the browser tab; we do not use persistent tracking cookies here.
  • Contact us — reach out via GitHub Issues for privacy questions or requests.

Children

FF@15 is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. League of Legends is rated for teen audiences; parents and guardians should supervise minors' use of online games and third-party tools.

International visitors

Kickback, LLC is based in the United States. If you access FF@15 or this site from outside the U.S., your information may be processed in the U.S. and other countries where our service providers operate.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Material changes may also be noted in release notes or on the home page.
