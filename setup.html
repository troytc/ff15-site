Setup guide

FF@15 runs beside the League client on your machine. No Riot API key, no account, no ads. Install, log into League, and the overlay connects automatically.

Download FF@15 Latest release

01Install

Grab the latest build for your platform from the download section or GitHub Releases:

  • Windows — .exe or .msi installer
  • macOS — universal .app.zip (Apple Silicon + Intel)

First launch (unsigned builds)

Installers are not code-signed yet:

  • macOS — right-click the app → Open the first time
  • Windows — at SmartScreen, choose More info → Run anyway

The app can auto-update when a newer build is published. Check Settings → Updates inside FF@15 for status.

02Launch League

Open the League client and log in on the same machine. FF@15 connects on startup and follows gameflow — lobby, champ select, in game, and post-game.

League must be installed and logged in locally. FF@15 reads from the official League Client (LCU) and Live Client Data APIs.

03Configure overlays

Open Overlay Settings in the app (title bar icon) to toggle what appears in game:

  • Live stats bar — CS/m, Vis/m, G/m, gold vs lane, gold vs team, KP, and jungle first-clear time
  • Lane quest — compact chip with your lane opponent's role-quest progress
  • Death recap — left sidebar while dead (placeholder item/coach content today)

Previews show sample data before you queue, so you can dial in layout without loading into a match.

04Play & review

During a game, the live stats bar and companion scoreboard track CS/m, gold pace, vision, KP, and gold swings vs lane and team. Hold Tab to peek the stats bar.

After the game, match history and ranked LP tracking refresh when you leave post-game. Captured timelines link to history rows when match capture data exists.

Where data is saved

  • Windows — %APPDATA%\Roaming\com.ff15gg.app\
  • macOS — ~/Library/Application Support/com.ff15gg.app/

Match events and history cache live under match_events/.
